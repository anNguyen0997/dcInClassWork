About Me

My Name is An Nguyen and while road tripping though the west coast, my family and I stopped by Lake Tahoe. It is a beautiful lake near the border of California and Nevada. The water is as blue as the sky, the trees were tall, green, and surrounded the lake, and sand was dry and warm. The view from the Lake Tahoe beach was incredible. On the horizon, you can see there are mountain ranges covered in snow, forests covered the lake's perimeter, all while being on the beach. I was extremely lucky and grateful to have seen such a sight.
